WHO WE ARE

A Voice of Hope
for Every Ear

Cup of Hope FM is a 24/7 community radio station powered by faith, music, and the relentless belief that every person deserves encouragement. Born from a heart to serve, we broadcast uplifting messages, live worship, prayer, and the best in gospel and inspirational music across South Africa and beyond.

From our humble beginnings to a growing online presence, our mission remains unchanged — to pour out hope into hearts, homes, and communities one broadcast at a time.
